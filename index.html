About Club

Entrepreneurship is not only about having great business ideas but the real challenge is about presenting how we can impact people’s lives. Unfortunately, many students lack the skill of presenting their ideas. Here, at the Entrepreneur’s Club, CMRCET, we help the students gain the right knowledge and skills to present their ideas, implement them, and turn their dream of being an entrepreneur into reality.

The students will be imparted with the knowledge towards their entrepreneurial voyage to face all the hindrances and push start their start-ups. Students with scalable ideas will be given the Strategies to scale their businesses to turn them into million-dollar businesses and a unicorn. Students will have the access to knowledge regarding marketing strategies, business plans, and Financial management skills whether it may be regarding Financial management in their life or Financial management regarding their startup, students will be given detailed guidance on Financial Management.

E-summit 2k23

“E-Summit 2k23, Be a hustler” connects business leaders, innovators, and visionaries. It provides an opportunity for entrepreneurs to network and connect with other like-minded individuals, which can lead to valuable partnerships and collaborations. Additionally, the summit features guest speakers, workshops, and many more, which provide valuable insights and knowledge to help entrepreneurs like many of you grow their businesses. Some important features of this e-summit include:
• Networking opportunities: the rising minds will have the chance to meet and connect with other business owners, potential partners, and investors. • Guest speakers: successful entrepreneurs provide valuable insights and inspiration for attendees.
• Interactive workshops: Hands-on workshops or breakout sessions will provide u with practical skills and knowledge that u can apply to your businesses.
• Mentorship: It Provides opportunities for you to connect with experienced entrepreneurs who can serve as mentors which can be a valuable resource.
• Access to resources: Having access to resources such as funding information, legal advice, and marketing tips can be helpful for entrepreneurs.
